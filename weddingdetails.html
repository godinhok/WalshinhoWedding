Ceremony Details

  • Location: Kilnaspic Church, Mooncoin
  • Time: 13:30
  • Date: 27th May 2016

The wedding ceremony will take place in Kilnaspic Church, a picturesque location located in the parish of Mooncoin. This is Kate's local church and she served Mass there as a child. Ceremony music will be provided by Something Blue Acoustics. Please see map for directions.

Reception Details

  • Location: Brandon House Hotel, New Ross
  • Time: 15:30 (approx)
  • Date: 27th May 2016

The wedding ceremony will be followed by a reception at Brandon House Hotel, New Ross. There will be a drinks reception on arrival, followed by a four course meal. Later in the evening, we have organised a band and DJ so we can dance and be merry until late into the night! Please see map for directions from Kilnaspic Church to Brandon House Hotel.

After-party

  • Location: Godinho family home, Ardera, Mooncoin
  • Time: 15:00
  • Date: 28th May 2016

Celebrations will continue on 28th May with a party at Kate's family home. We have organised a marquee in the back garden and there will be a barbecue followed by a hog roast later in the evening. We have also organised a local band to come and play. Please see map for directions to the house.
