Kilnaspic Church

Location

The church is located about 6 km from the village of Mooncoin, approximately two hours drive from Dublin and about half an hour's drive from Brandon House Hotel.

Driving Directions from M9

From roundabout at the end of M9, take the N24 (Limerick road) as far as Mooncoin. In the village, turn right beside Comerford's pub. Turn left at the crossroads at the bottom. Continue on this road for about 3.4 km until you reach a crossroads. Turn left and continue to next right turn with signpost for church. Go up the road and turn right at the top. The car park is on the left and the church is straight ahead. There is more parking along the road beyond the church. It's about 6 km from Mooncoin and should take about 10 minutes from there.

Brandon House Hotel

Location

Brandon House Hotel is located in New Ross, Co. Wexford, about 2 hours drive from Dublin, and half an hour from Kilnaspic Church.

Driving Directions

From, Dublin, New Ross can be reached via the M9. Please use the Google map on the right to get exact directions to the hotel from your starting point.

Public Transport

New Ross is reachable by bus from Dublin - please see here for timetables. Lifts may be available from the hotel to the church on the morning of the wedding - please get in touch with us if you need a lift.

Kate's Family Home

Location

Kate's family home is located approximately 3 miles from the village of Mooncoin.

Driving Directions to Mooncoin

Take the N24 (Limerick road) as far as Mooncoin. In the village, turn right beside Comerford's pub. Take a right at the next crossroads. Continue on this road until you reach a T junction. Take a right down the hill and continue up the road until you reach the first house on the left.
